Tinatin Zeragia

Associate Professor at the British Teaching University in Georgia

Tinatin Zeragia is an Associate Professor at the British Teaching University in Georgia. She as well is the Head of the Business and Management Programme.Assoc. Prof. T. Zeragia has published several articles about current management topics and taken part in scientific conferences based on her research interests, including The 12th International RAIS Conference on Social Sciences and Humanities held at Princeton, NJ, USA. Assoc. Prof. T. Zeragia has many years of experience in teaching at different levels of business studies (BBA, MBA). She has been involved in the preparation process of several training programmes and curricula in the field of management. Assoc. Prof. T. Zeragia has more than ten years of working experience in the banking sector of Georgia.
